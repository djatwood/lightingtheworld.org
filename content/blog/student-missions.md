Stomping on mud, hiking on mountains and mixing cement are all common duties for a typical mission trip at VIDA Internacional, Honduras. During their nine-week internship, the Matteson students also had to face other challenges like getting used to cold showers, beans and rice &amp; rice and beans, sleeping under mosquito-nets, and dealing with a lot of Spanish speaking people with a very different understanding of life&#8230;

The classes &#8216;Leadership, Total Quality, Financial Management and HR Management&#8217; given by the VIDA staff were also a great blessing.&#8221; All these proved to be educational, transforming and refining to each missionary.

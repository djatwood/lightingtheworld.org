*This is told in the perspective of Dr. Angie Sanchez, our LIGHT Director for Peru.










We conducted a LIGHT one month course in the Avenida Brasil SDA Church in Lima, Peru during the month of May, with 65 precious graduates. Here is a report from Dr. Angie Sanchez, our LIGHT Director for Peru:

The Lord continually opens doors for LIGHT in Lima, the beautiful capital of Peru. This time, it happened at the SDA church of Brazil Avenue, a large and active church. From May 1st through the 26th, a group of about 67 people attended the course. The participants were mostly from the vicinity of the church, although some had to make long commutes to attend the course. The interesting thing about the group, was that, several of the attendees were people who do not attend the Adventist Church, but who were very interested in the topics provided.

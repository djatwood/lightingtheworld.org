Recently a LIGHT Health Expo has been held in Tunisia, a small predominantly muslim country located in north Africa. During the event, held inside a famous restaurant in Algeri (the capital of the country), almost 100 people have been reached and 70 of those have successfully quit smoking, an amazing result!

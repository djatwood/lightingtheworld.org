### Scrambled Tofu

This scrambled tofu recipe is included in our Caribbean Cookbook (coming soon) and is also included in our [Spanish Cookbook (currently available in Spanish)](http://lightingtheworld.org/training/dvd-training/).

**Ingredients:**

*   1 cups firm tofu, crumbled
*   1 big onion
*   2 tomatoes
*   6 garlic cloves
*   1 red pepper
*   1 tsp soy sauce
*   Salt to taste
*   Cilantro (optional)

**Procedure**

In a pan with little oil fry the tofu for 5 minutes.  

Prepare a good sauce with the other ingredients, add the tofu and stir constantly, cook for 10 min. Sprinkle with soy sauce, cilantro, and salt.

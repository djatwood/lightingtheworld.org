### GARLIC MAYO

**Ingredients:**

*   4-6 cloves garlic
*   1 pound firm tofu
*   1/2 cup lemon juice
*   Salt to taste
*   1/2 cup olive oil

**Procedure:**  

Blend all ingredients together until light and fluffy except the oil.  

Turn blender on low setting and gradually add oil.

Uchee Pines Lifestyle Center offers a 17-day residential experience in health education and lifestyle change. Our goal is to educate and empower individuals to live lifestyles for optimal physical, mental, and spiritual health. Our motto — Improving Health One Choice At A Time — emphasizes our passion to help you achieve this goal. Come join us and renew your health — for good!

Uchee Pines Institute is in the Eastern Time Zone, and is situated on 200 acres in Central Alabama, near the Georgia border. We are 30 minutes from Columbus, Georgia, and 2 ½ hours from Atlanta, Georgia. Nestled amongst the pine trees and gentle rolling hills, with abundant opportunities to enjoy the birds, squirrels, and white-tailed deer, our campus provides the peace of natural, outdoor living.

## What Uchee Pines Is Doing

Training those that have a desire to serve the Lord in medical missionary lines by providing them with practical health evangelism tools and giving them a strong biblical and true scientific foundation for a lifetime of service.

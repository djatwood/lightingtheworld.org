## Overview

We started medical ministry in Central Asia in 2004, first in Tajikistan, than in Uzbekistan, Kirgizstan. In those countries a number of people that has being trained in Medical-Missionary schools, and some of them working at Health rooms. This Medical missionary ministry, the only ministry that can be used in most of those countries as Evangelistic tool. In 2012 we have being invited to do a ministry in Kazakhstan.

## What We Are Doing

We have a health room in A big city Almaty, where we have people coming for health consultations and treatments. Also here we have being doing a Lifestyle sessions, Where people come for 10 days, they live with us, going to morning devotional, treatments breakfast, lunch, walks… Making friends with people and helping them physically and spiritually. Prayer and dream to build a Lifestyle Center in Central Asia!
